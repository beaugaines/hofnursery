Deep End Butter
  • TYPE: Sativa
  • LINEAGE: Peanut Butter Breath x Deep End Purple (GDP bx)
  • FLOWER TIME: 7 weeks
  • VEGETATIVE TIME: Fast
  • FLAVOR: Spice, Earth, Skunk, Gas
  • YIELD: High
  • POTENCY: 28%-32%
  • BREEDER: Oakland Seed Co
  • NOTES: Deep End Butter is a fast-finishing, high yielding Sativa that turns a deep purple in any climate. With its unusual presence of lesser-known terpenes such as bisabolol and nerolidol, its distinctive profile of roasted nuts, gas and earthy spice stand out in the crowd of Gelato crosses. Ranging between 28% and 32% thc, it hits hard and fast. It can be harvested in 7 weeks but for more gas it can go an extra week without losing its luster
