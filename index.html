House of Flavor Nursery provides clean and pristine California licensed cannabis and licensed hemp clones of high-demand, verified genetics. We also provide high THCa varieties and can ship globally. Operating in Oakland California since 2016, HOF has ground-zero access to the most sought after commercial cuts on the market today

White Cherry Gelato 33
  • TYPE: Hybrid
  • LINEAGE: Unknown
  • FLOWER TIME: 8-9 weeks
  • VEGETATIVE TIME: Fast
  • FLAVOR: Extra candy on the classic sweet and gassy Gelato
  • YIELD: Medium high
  • POTENCY: High
  • BREEDER: Sherbinsky
  • NOTES: This cut has been circulating for some time in California as Gelato 33. Although it is likely the original Gelato 33, it pops with so much candy that it is often renamed a Runtz or LCG to reflect that profile. One common rename in the traditional market is White Cherry Runtz. We’ve taken the liberty to combine that name with the name originally given by its legendary breeder.

Apple Mochi Gelato (K47)
  • TYPE: Hybrid
  • LINEAGE: Unknown
  • FLOWER TIME: 8-9 weeks
  • VEGETATIVE TIME: Medium
  • FLAVOR: Unique candy/gas with an unusual fruit back
  • YIELD: Medium high
  • POTENCY: High
  • BREEDER: Unknown
  • NOTES: This cut came to the nursery labeled only K47. Typically we don’t include unverified genetics on our menu, but after testing this one, we made an exception.
